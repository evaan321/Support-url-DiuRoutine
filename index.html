Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why can’t I find my section’s routine?

    Ensure you’ve entered a valid section code (e.g., “61_N”) in the Student tab. Check your internet connection and try again.

  • Why are no empty rooms showing?

    Try selecting a different time slot or day in the Empty Room tab, as availability varies based on DIU’s schedule.

  • How do I contact a teacher?

    Enter the teacher’s initials (e.g., “MM”) in the Teacher Info tab to view their email or phone details.

  • What days are included in the schedule?

    The app covers Saturday to Thursday, as Friday is excluded per DIU’s academic calendar.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 26, 2025

DIU Routine Viewer, developed by Md Abdullah Rahman Evaan, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

DIU Routine Viewer does not collect or store personal data. The app allows users to input section codes (e.g., “61_N”) or teacher initials (e.g., “MM”) to fetch class schedules, empty room availability, or teacher profiles from Daffodil International University’s servers. This data is not stored locally or transmitted to third parties.

How We Use Information

The section codes and teacher initials you enter are used solely to query DIU’s servers for relevant schedule or profile information. No personal information is collected, stored, or shared by the app.

Third-Party Services

The app communicates with DIU’s servers to retrieve schedule and profile data. We do not control these servers, and their data handling is governed by DIU’s privacy policies.

Data Security

Since no personal data is stored by the app, there is no risk of data breaches from our end. We use standard security practices to ensure the app operates safely.
