Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why can’t I find my section’s routine?

    Ensure you’ve entered a valid section code (e.g., “61_N”) in the Student tab. Check your internet connection and try again.

  • Why are no empty rooms showing?

    Try selecting a different time slot or day in the Empty Room tab, as availability varies based on DIU’s schedule.

  • How do I contact a teacher?

    Enter the teacher’s initials (e.g., “MM”) in the Teacher Info tab to view their email or phone details.

  • What days are included in the schedule?

    The app covers Saturday to Thursday, as Friday is excluded per DIU’s academic calendar.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 26, 2025

DIU Routine Viewer, developed by Md Abdullah Rahman Evaan, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle information when you use our app, ensuring transparency and compliance with applicable regulations.

Information We Collect

DIU Routine Viewer does not collect, store, or process personal data. The app allows users to input section codes (e.g., “61_N”) or teacher initials (e.g., “MM”) to retrieve class schedules, empty room availability, or teacher profiles from Daffodil International University’s servers. These inputs are used solely for querying and are not stored locally or shared with third parties.

How We Use Information

Section codes and teacher initials entered in the app are used exclusively to fetch relevant data from DIU’s servers. No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted beyond this purpose.

Third-Party Services

The app communicates with Daffodil International University’s servers to retrieve schedule and profile information. Data handling by these servers is governed by DIU’s privacy policies, which are outside our control.

Data Security

As the app does not store personal data, there is no risk of data breaches from our application. We implement industry-standard security practices to ensure safe operation of the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. The latest version will always be available on this page.
